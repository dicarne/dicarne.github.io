## 积分变换

$$\int xe^xdx=\int (xe^x+e^x-e^x)dx=(x-1)e^x$$

$$\int \frac{dx}{\sin x}=\int \frac{dx}{2\sin \frac{x}{2}\cos \frac{x}{2}}=\int \frac{\sec^2 \frac{x}{2} d \frac{x}{2}}{\tan \frac{x}{2}}=\int \frac{d\tan \frac{x}{2}}{\tan \frac{x}{2}}=\ln (\tan \frac{x}{2})+C$$
